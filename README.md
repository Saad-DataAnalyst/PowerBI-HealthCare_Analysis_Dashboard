# PowerBI-HealthCare_Analysis_Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Healthcare Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI. The dashboard analyzes patient data, hospital performance, and financial metrics to provide actionable insights for decision-making.
---
## Objectives
- Analyze patient trends and hospital performance
- Monitor revenue and financial metrics
- Identify key medical conditions and patient demographics
- Perform month-over-month (MoM) analysis
---
## Dataset Features
- Patient demographics (Age, Gender, Blood Type)
- Medical conditions
- Admission & discharge dates
- Billing amount
- Hospital & doctor information
- Insurance provider
- Admission type
---
## Dashboard Pages

### Patient Analysis
- Total Patients KPI
- Trend Analysis (Monthly Patients)
- Gender Distribution
- Age Group Analysis
- Patients by Medical Condition
---
### Financial Analysis
- Total Revenue KPI
- Revenue Trend Analysis
- Revenue by Insurance Provider
- Revenue by Medical Condition
- Admission Type Analysis
- Length of Stay Analysis
---
## Key Insights
- Significant drop in patient volume and revenue in May
- Chronic diseases like Diabetes and Hypertension dominate
- Senior patients represent the largest age group
- Majority of patients fall under long-stay category
- Insurance providers contribute significantly to revenue
